WELCOME TO GTHub!









Welcome to GTHub! GTHub is a website specially designed for students at Georgia Tech to help better organize their classes. This website is specially made to be a forum for students to share notes and to collaborate in their classes to help them help one another. The functions on this website will group students in similar classes together. Students are able to upload or send their notes to other students who may have missed out on attending class. Students also have the option to collaborate on notes together and update one single document for the class. In addition, GTHub provides a quiz service that generates questions to help the students prepare for assessments in the class. Although still in the preliminary stages of development, we hope that the website will gradually grow to become a widely used forum for all students. Our goal is to create a efficient environment to help make class organization easier.
